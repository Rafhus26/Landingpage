What I Offer

Loan Programs for Every Buyer

From first-time buyers to seasoned investors, I offer a wide range of loan products tailored to your unique financial situation.

🏠
Conventional Loans

Flexible financing for qualified buyers with competitive rates and a variety of down payment options for primary, second, and investment properties.

🌟
FHA Loans

Government-backed loans ideal for first-time homebuyers with lower credit score requirements and low minimum down payment requirements.

🎉
VA Loans

Exclusive benefits for eligible veterans, active-duty service members, and surviving spouses — including no down payment options.

🌱
USDA Loans

Zero down payment financing for eligible rural and suburban homebuyers backed by the U.S. Department of Agriculture.

📉
Refinancing

Lower your rate, reduce your term, or access home equity with a refinance strategy tailored to your financial goals.

💲
Jumbo Loans

Financing solutions for higher-priced properties that exceed conventional conforming loan limits, with personalized guidance.

📈
DSCR Loans

Debt Service Coverage Ratio loans for real estate investors — qualify based on property rental income rather than personal income, making it easier to grow your portfolio.

🏠
Reverse Mortgage

Allow eligible homeowners aged 62+ to convert home equity into tax-free funds with no required monthly mortgage payments — while continuing to live in your home.

🏭
Bank Statement Loans

Ideal for self-employed borrowers — qualify using 12–24 months of bank statements instead of traditional tax returns, making homeownership accessible for entrepreneurs.
